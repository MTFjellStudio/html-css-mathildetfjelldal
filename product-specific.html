Designed in a timeless fit with minimalist Scandinavian detailing, it keeps you dry, comfortable, and ready for anything the weather brings.

Features
  • Waterproof and windproof design
  • Breathable 3-layer shell construction
  • Adjustable hood and cuffs

Impact
  • Made with recycled polyester and eco-friendly water-repellent coating (PFC-free).
  • Designed for performance without compromising environmental responsibility.
Technology
  • Advanced membrane technology provides 20,000mm waterproofing and 15,000g/m² breathability for optimal comfort during active use.
